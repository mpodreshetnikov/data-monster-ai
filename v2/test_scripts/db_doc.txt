The database contains information about the users of cash registers, which are installed in the pharmacies "Губернские аптеки".
For the management of the "Губернские аптеки", cash register users are employees.
To get cashiers/employees in a pharmacy, use the "uas_cash_session_doc" table. It is important to note that the columns: "owner_key" is the pharmacy key, and "user_id" is the employee ID.

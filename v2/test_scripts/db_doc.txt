The database contains information about the users of cash registers, which are installed in the pharmacies "Gubernskie Apteki".
For the management of the "Gubernskie Apteki", cash register users are employees.
To get cashiers/employees by a pharmacy, use the "uas_cash_session_doc" table. It is important to note that the columns: "owner_key" is the pharmacy key, and "user_id" is the employee ID.
